# Electricity & Early/Late Stay

- Electricity is included during your booked stay (check-in → check-out).
- Early check-in and late check-out may be possible if the schedule allows.
- No extra charge for early baggage drop-off or late baggage pickup.
- If you stay longer and use electricity (AC, lights, appliances), a fee of PHP 10 per hour applies; fees processed through Airbnb.
