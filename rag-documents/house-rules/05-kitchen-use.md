# Kitchen Use / Cooking Policy

- Clean as you go: wash dishes and wipe surfaces.
- Turn off the AC when cooking to avoid steam buildup and odors.
- Ventilate: open windows and use the range hood when cooking.
- Avoid strong-smelling foods: dried fish, fermented foods (bagoong, kimchi), curry, heavily seasoned grilled meats.
- Limit cooking to quick/simple meals (e.g., boiled/fried/scrambled eggs, lightly fried Spam or meatloaf).
- For heavier or longer cooking, use the shared kitchen downstairs (available until 9:00 PM).
- If cooking causes strong odors or damage requiring professional cleaning, costs may be charged via Airbnb.
