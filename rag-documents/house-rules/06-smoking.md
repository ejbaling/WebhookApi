# Smoking

- No smoking indoors anywhere on the property.
- Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor smoking areas.
- Applies to cigarettes, cigars, pipes and vaping/e‑cigarette devices.
- Please extinguish and dispose of cigarette butts and vaping waste in outdoor bins or ashtrays provided.
- Example user questions this covers: "Can I smoke?", "Is smoking allowed?", "Where can I smoke?"
