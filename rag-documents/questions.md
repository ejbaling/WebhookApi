# Guest Questions & Answers

## 1. Who can stay at the property?

- Maximum of 2 registered guests only.
- Children under 13 are **not allowed** due to unit being on the 2nd floor and windows having no security bars.
- Up to 2 approved visitors are allowed between 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM.
- Total persons in the room must not exceed 4 at any time.
- No overnight visitors allowed.

## 2. What are the check-in and check-out times?

- Check-in: 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM (slightly flexible, but very late check-ins cannot be accommodated)
- Check-out: 12:00 PM
- Early check-in or late check-out may be possible if the schedule allows; a small fee of PHP 10/hour applies if electricity or AC is used outside booked hours.

## 3. Are meals provided?

- Meals are **not provided**.
- The kitchen is stocked with basic utensils to prepare **snacks or light meals**.

## 4. Can I use the kitchen?

- Guests may use the **kitchenette inside the unit**.
- **Clean as you go**: wash dishes and wipe surfaces.
- **Turn off the AC while cooking** to avoid mold, bacteria, and odors.
- **Ventilate properly**: open windows and use the range hood.
- Avoid cooking strong-smelling foods such as:
  - Dried fish
  - Fermented foods (bagoong, kimchi)
  - Curry
  - Grilled meats with heavy seasoning
- Limit cooking time and methods: quick and simple meals only.
  - Acceptable: boiled/fried/scrambled eggs, lightly fried Spam or meatloaf
- For longer meals, use the **shared kitchen downstairs** (available until 9:00 PM).

## 5. Are pets allowed?

- Unfortunately, **no pets** are allowed inside the property.

## 6. Can I smoke?

- **No smoking indoors.**
- Please use designated outdoor areas and dispose of cigarette butts responsibly.

## 7. Are parties allowed?

- **No parties or events**.
- The property is for registered guests only.

## 8. What are the quiet hours?

- 10:00 PM – 8:00 AM.
- Keep noise, music, and conversation low to respect neighbors.

## 9. Is Wi-Fi available?

- Yes, Wi-Fi is provided in each room via a wired connection.
- Each room is connected to a **central gigabit switch** through a **CAT6 cable terminated at a 2.4GHz access point**.
- 100 Mbps is allocated per room.
- Guests may replace the access point at their own expense.
- Wired connection is prioritized for maximum speed; speed is not guaranteed at all times.

## 10. Is laundry available?

- Washing machine and clothes dryer are available.
- Can be turned on/off via the website using your email as credentials.
- Bills are sent to your email after appliance use.
- **Operating hours:** 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM daily.

## 11. How do I access my room and the property?

- Rooms are on the 2nd floor.
- Guests are provided a **PIN code** for the smartlock.
- The property gate and rooms use standard keys for security.

## 12. What about parking?

- **Covered parking** for 2 small cars (first-come, first-served).
- Garage is elevated; drive slowly to avoid scraping the bottom of your car.
- Plenty of street parking available if garage is occupied.
- Do not block neighbors or other guests.

## 13. What security measures are in place?

- Outdoor cameras monitor:
  - Garage & front gate
  - East side of the house
  - West lanai area
  - Back of the house (fire exit stair)
- No cameras face indoor spaces or private guest areas.

## 14. Is the property safe during the rainy season?

- From July–October, heavy rain may cause temporary street flooding up to 20 cm.
- Usually clears overnight; severe cases may take 2–3 days.
- Vehicle access may be limited.
- House remains safe and dry.
- Private vehicle assistance available for transport if needed.

## 15. How far are nearby locations?

- **Iloilo International Airport:** 24 min by car, 16.6 km
- **Vista Mall:** 7 min by car, 2.7 km
- **Iloilo Supermart:** 9 min by car, 2.6 km
- **Festive Walk Mall:** 11 min by car, 3.4 km
- **SM City Iloilo:** 14 min by car, 4.5 km
- **Iloilo City Proper:** 21 min by car, 7.7 km
- **7-Eleven:** 10 min walk, 700 meters

## 16. What happens if I damage property or appliances?

- Guests are responsible for any damage.
- Report issues immediately.
- Strong odors from cooking may incur **deep cleaning charges** if necessary.
