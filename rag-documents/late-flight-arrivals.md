# Late Flight Arrivals

## Guest Question

Hello! Good afternoon. Our trip to Iloilo has been delayed.  
We’ll be expecting to check in by 6 PM instead. Is that okay?

## Response

No worries, we consider check-in outside our standard check-in time due to flight delays.

## Notes

- Our standard check-in time is 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM.
- For guests affected by flight delays, we are flexible and allow arrivals after 5:00 PM.
