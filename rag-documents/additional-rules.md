# Additional Rules

## Child Policy

Strictly no children under 13 years old.  
The unit is located on the 2nd floor and the windows do not have security bars. Because of this design, the accommodation is not suitable for young children. We are unable to accept bookings with children, and exceptions cannot be made.

## Meals

We don’t provide meals, but the kitchen is stocked with basic utensils so you can prepare snacks or light meals during your stay.

## Check-In / Check-Out

Check-in time is 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM, and check-out time is 12:00 PM.

Our check-in time is slightly flexible, but very late check-ins cannot be accommodated. This is to ensure the safety and comfort of all our guests and to respect the needs of our staff. Please arrive within the specified time frame.

## Electricity & Early/Late Stay Policy

- Electricity is included during your booked stay (from check-in to check-out).
- Early check-in and late check-out may be possible if the schedule allows.
- No extra charge if:
  - You check out earlier than the booked check-out time.
  - You are only dropping off bags before check-in.
  - You are only picking up bags after check-out.

If you wish to stay longer and use electricity (AC, lights, appliances), a small fee of PHP 10 per hour applies. Fees are processed through Airbnb (no cash payments).

## Kitchen Use Policy (Strictly Enforced)

Please read carefully before using the kitchen.  
Feel free to use the kitchenette inside the unit. Follow these guidelines to maintain a clean and comfortable space.

### Clean As You Go

Wash all dishes, wipe down surfaces, and leave the kitchen as clean as you found it.

### Turn Off the AC When Cooking

Cooking with the AC on may cause steam buildup that encourages mold and bacteria, and can cause odors in the AC unit requiring deep cleaning.

### Ventilate Properly

Open windows and turn on the range hood before cooking.

### Avoid Cooking Strong-Smelling Foods

Avoid foods with strong odors, including:

- Dried fish
- Fermented foods (e.g., bagoong, kimchi)
- Curry
- Grilled meats with heavy seasoning

### Limit Cooking Time & Methods

Keep cooking quick and simple. Avoid long cooking times or heavy frying (e.g., fried fish, hotdogs, stir-fries).

Acceptable quick meals:

- Boiled, fried, or scrambled eggs
- Spam or meatloaf (lightly fried)

For other meals, use the **shared kitchen downstairs** (available until 9:00 PM).

### Important Notice

If strong odors require professional deodorizing or deep cleaning, or appliances are damaged due to prolonged/improper cooking, costs may be charged based on service provider quotes.
