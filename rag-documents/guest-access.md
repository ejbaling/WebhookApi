# Guest Access

## Wifi

Each room is connected to a central gigabit switch through a CAT6 cable terminated with a 2.4GHz access point. 100MBps is allocated for each room.

Guests are allowed to replace the access point at their own expense. We cannot guarantee 100Mbps all the time, but we've rolled out wired instead of Wifi/Mesh connection for each room to ensure we deliver the maximum consistent speed provided by our ISP.

## Laundry

Washing machine and clothes dryer are available.

These appliances can be turned on/off from our website using your email address as your access credentials. Usage bill/cost is sent to your email address after the device is turned off.

To ensure a peaceful and comfortable stay for everyone, appliances are set to operate daily from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Thank you for your understanding!

## TV

Netflix, YouTube, and local channels are available.

## Smartlock

Guests will be provided with a PIN code to access the 2nd floor where the rooms are.

The property gate and guest rooms are secured by standard keys.

## Parking

Parking availability depends on the number of rooms occupied during your stay:

- The place has covered parking for 2 small cars
- The garage is elevated higher than the street level, so we advise you to slow down to avoid scraping the bottom of your car
- The previous guest had a stock Mitsubishi Mirage G4 (not lowered) and could park with no issues
- There are plenty of street parking spaces outside the property if the garage is already occupied
