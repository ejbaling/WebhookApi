# House Rules

## Check In / Check Out

Check-in: 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM  
Check-out: 12:00 PM

Very late check-ins cannot be accommodated.

## Electricity Policy

Electricity is included only during the booked stay period.

Early check-in or late check-out may be possible depending on availability.

Guests are not charged if they:

- Drop off bags before check-in
- Pick up bags after check-out
- Leave earlier than scheduled

If guests stay longer and use electricity, a fee of PHP 10 per hour will apply.

Payments are processed through Airbnb only.

## Smoking

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the property.

Please use designated outdoor areas.

## Pets

Pets are not allowed.

## Quiet Hours

Quiet hours are from:

10:00 PM – 8:00 AM

Please keep noise levels low to respect neighbors and other guests.

## Parties and Events

Parties and events are not allowed.

## Visitors

Maximum of 2 visitors at a time.

Visiting hours:
8:00 AM – 9:00 PM.

No overnight visitors.

Total persons inside the room must not exceed 4.

## Damages

Guests are responsible for any damage caused to the property.

## Garbage and Recycling

Dispose of garbage and recycling using the designated bins.

## Security

Always lock doors and windows when leaving.

Report any security concerns immediately.

## Lost Items

We are not responsible for items left behind.

## WiFi Usage

Guests must use the WiFi responsibly and avoid illegal downloads.

## Energy Use

Please turn off air conditioning and appliances when leaving the room.

## Parking

Parking is first-come, first-served.

Do not block driveways or access areas for neighbors or other guests.
