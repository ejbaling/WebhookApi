# Other Details to Note

## Kitchen Use Policy (Strictly Enforced)

Please read carefully before using the kitchen.

Feel free to use the kitchenette inside the unit for convenience. We kindly ask that you follow these guidelines to help maintain a clean and comfortable space for you and future guests.

### Clean As You Go

Please wash all dishes, wipe down surfaces, and leave the kitchen just as clean as you found it.

### Turn Off the Air Conditioner When Cooking

Cooking while the air conditioner is on can cause steam buildup that encourages mold and bacteria growth. This may affect health and can also cause odors to circulate through the AC unit, requiring deep cleaning.

### Ventilate Properly

Always open the windows and turn on the range hood before cooking.

### Avoid Cooking Strong-Smelling Foods

To keep the space fresh for everyone, please avoid cooking foods that produce strong or lingering odors, including:

- Dried fish
- Fermented foods (e.g., bagoong, kimchi)
- Curry
- Grilled meats with heavy seasoning

### Limit Cooking Time and Methods

Please keep cooking quick and simple in the kitchenette. Avoid dishes that require long cooking times or heavy frying (for example fried fish, hotdogs, or stir-fries).

Acceptable quick meals include:

- Boiled, fried, or scrambled eggs
- Spam or meatloaf (lightly fried)

For other meals, you may use the **shared kitchen downstairs**, available until **9:00 PM**.

### Important Notice

If strong odors require professional deodorizing or deep cleaning (for example curtains or furniture), or if appliances are damaged due to prolonged or improper cooking, the cost may be charged based on the service provider’s quote.

## Power Outage Notice

In the event of a power failure, the property has a backup generator that powers most appliances, including:

- Air conditioner
- Electric stove
- Refrigerator

The generator does **not** have an Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS). There may be a delay of **2–5 minutes** before appliances can be turned on.

## Child Safety Notice

The accommodation is **not suitable for children**.

The units are located on the **2nd floor**, and the windows do **not have security bars**. For safety reasons, the property is not a good fit for children under **13 years old**.
